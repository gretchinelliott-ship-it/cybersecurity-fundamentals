## What is Cybersecurity?
Cybersecurity is the practice of protecting systems, networks,
and data from digital attacks, unauthorized access, and damage.

## CIA Triad
The CIA Triad is the foundation of information security.

- Confidentiality: Ensuring information is only accessible to authorized users
- Integrity: Ensuring data is accurate and not altered without authorization
- Availability: Ensuring systems and data are accessible when needed

## Threat
A threat is any potential danger that could exploit a vulnerability
and cause harm to a system.

## Vulnerability
A vulnerability is a weakness in a system that can be exploited
by a threat.

## Why Cybersecurity Matters
- Protects personal and sensitive data
- Prevents financial loss
- Maintains trust in systems and organizations
